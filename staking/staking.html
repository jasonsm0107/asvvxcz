Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our staking platform.

What is staking?

+
Staking is a process where you lock your cryptocurrency assets for a specific period to support the operations of a blockchain network. In return, you receive rewards in the form of additional tokens. It's similar to earning interest in a traditional bank account, but typically offers higher yields. Wave Finance provides various staking options with different risk levels and reward structures to match your investment preferences.

How are staking rewards calculated?

+
Staking rewards are calculated based on several factors including the amount staked, the duration of staking, the specific pool's APY (Annual Percentage Yield), and the network's overall staking participation. The APY shown for each pool represents the annualized return you can expect if you stake for a full year. Rewards are typically distributed on a daily or weekly basis, depending on the pool you choose.

Is there a minimum amount required to stake?

+
Yes, most staking pools have a minimum staking requirement to ensure efficient operation and security of the network. The minimum amount varies depending on the pool and token type. For WAVE single staking, the minimum amount is 10 WAVE. For LP token staking, the minimum is the equivalent of $50 in LP tokens. These requirements help prevent spam transactions and ensure participants have a meaningful stake in the network's success.

Can I withdraw my staked tokens before the lock period ends?

+
Yes, you can withdraw your staked tokens before the lock period ends, but an early withdrawal fee will be applied. This fee varies depending on the staking pool and how early you withdraw. For most pools, the fee ranges from 3% to 10% of your staked amount. The fee is designed to encourage long-term staking and maintain the stability of the protocol. To avoid these fees, we recommend selecting a lock period that aligns with your investment timeline.

Are there any risks involved with staking?

+
While staking is generally considered less risky than active trading, there are still potential risks to be aware of. These include smart contract risks, impermanent loss (for LP staking), market volatility, and regulatory changes. Wave Finance conducts regular security audits and implements robust security measures to minimize these risks, but it's important to understand that all crypto investments carry some level of risk. We recommend diversifying your portfolio and only staking amounts you can afford to lock for the selected period.

How can I maximize my staking rewards?

+
To maximize your staking rewards, consider strategies like selecting longer lock periods for higher APYs, participating in special staking events, compounding your rewards by re-staking them, diversifying across different pools, and staying informed about protocol updates and governance proposals. Additionally, holding vxWAVE tokens can boost your staking rewards by up to 2.5x in certain pools. Our analytics dashboard provides valuable insights to help you optimize your staking strategy based on market conditions and historical performance data.
